Privacy Policy

How MSC2OSC handles your data

Overview

MSC2OSC is designed with privacy in mind. The app operates entirely on your local device and does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

Data Collection

MSC2OSC does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No user accounts or registration
  • No data transmitted to external servers
  • No advertising or third-party services

Local Data Storage

MSC2OSC stores the following data locally on your device:

  • User Preferences: Settings such as your selected MIDI input device, OSC destination address and port, path template, and command filter preferences are stored locally using macOS UserDefaults.

Network Communication

MSC2OSC sends OSC messages over your local network to the destination you configure:

  • OSC messages are sent only to the IP address and port you specify
  • No data is sent to external servers or the internet
  • All network communication stays within your configured destination

MIDI Data

MSC2OSC processes MIDI Show Control (MSC) data locally on your device:

  • MSC messages are received from your selected MIDI input
  • Messages are translated to OSC and sent to your configured destination
  • No MIDI data is stored, logged, or transmitted externally

Support Requests

If you contact us through the support form on our website, we collect the information you provide (email address and message content) solely to respond to your inquiry. This information is processed through Formspree and is subject to their privacy policy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@kwallace.com.
